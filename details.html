Story of Covid-19 situation

16, Aug, 2021

The whole world, including Bangladesh, is going through a difficult time due to the corona epidemic. The first coronavirus patient was identified in Bangladesh on March 7, 2020. As a densely populated country, Bangladesh lacks awareness. The current lockdown situation is that poor families are living in extreme hardship. They have no food at home and cannot go to work. The number of such people is innumerable. Day laborer Madhu Sudan used to work in a garment factory. It was closed three months ago, now there is no work, she can ask someone for something out of shame. Many such workers, day laborers, low income people are spending their days without eating in the Corona epidemic.
