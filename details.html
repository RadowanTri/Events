About Bangabandhu Time Equality Convention (TEC)

Looking at the last ten years and the remarkable progress that Bangladesh has made in a wide range of fields, from economy to agriculture, women's empowerment to health and nutrition, from education to communication, we realize that the forces at work have indeed been activated a long time ago, in the years between 1972 and 1975. Those years set the benchmark, gave us a sense of direction and made it possible for us to imagine the impossible. With each passing year, that impossible seems a little less so because we draw our inspiration from the war of liberation and the legacy left behind by Bangabandhu Sheik Mujibur Rahman.
